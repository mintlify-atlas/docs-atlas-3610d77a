---
title: "Bundles"
description: "Browse featured bundle offers, see included skins, and check the total discounted bundle price."
---

The Bundles screen displays the currently featured bundles in the Valorant store. Each bundle groups a set of weapon skins together at a combined discounted price.

## What you see

For each active bundle, VShop shows:

- **Bundle artwork** — the bundle's promotional image displayed at the top.
- **Bundle name** — the name of the bundle.
- **Countdown timer** — time remaining until the bundle expires, shown alongside the bundle image.
- **Included skins** — each weapon skin in the bundle listed individually below the artwork.
- **Total price** — the combined discounted price of all items in the bundle (sum of individual `DiscountedPrice` values), displayed on the bundle header.

<Note>
  Multiple bundles can be active at the same time. VShop displays each one sequentially on the same scrollable screen.
</Note>

## When no bundle is active

If the Riot API returns no featured bundles for your account, VShop shows a question-mark icon and a message indicating there is no bundle currently available.

## Bundle pricing

The price shown is the total of all discounted item prices within the bundle, not the individual skin prices. Buying individual skins from a bundle outside of the bundle offer costs more.

<Info>
  Bundle items listed in VShop are the weapon skins included in the bundle. Non-skin items such as sprays or gun buddies that may be part of a bundle are not listed separately.
</Info>

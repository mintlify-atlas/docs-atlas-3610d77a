---
title: "Daily shop"
description: "Browse your four rotating daily skin offers, check VP prices, and preview skin levels and chromas."
---

The daily shop shows four weapon skins that rotate on a roughly 24-hour cycle. Each offer is fetched directly from Riot's storefront API and displayed with the skin image, name, and VP price.

## What you see

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Skin name" icon="tag">
    Displayed at the top of each card. Skins on your wishlist are marked with a ⭐ prefix.
  </Card>
  <Card title="VP price" icon="coins">
    The Valorant Points cost for each offer, shown beneath the skin name alongside the VP icon.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Skin image" icon="image">
    The skin's display icon rendered in the card cover area.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Countdown timer" icon="clock">
    Time remaining until the shop resets, shown in the top-right corner of the screen.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Viewing levels and chromas

Each skin card has two action buttons at the bottom:

- **Levels** — opens a media popup showing each upgrade level's display icon or streamed video.
- **Chromas** — opens a media popup showing each chroma variant's full render or streamed video.

Tap either button to cycle through the available variants for that skin.

## Countdown timer

The timer in the top-right corner counts down to the exact second when the shop refreshes. The remaining duration comes from the `SingleItemOffersRemainingDurationInSeconds` field returned by the Riot storefront endpoint.

<Note>
  Shop offers refresh roughly every 24 hours, but the exact reset time is tied to your account rather than a fixed wall-clock time. The countdown reflects the actual remaining duration for your account.
</Note>

## Wishlist indicator

If a skin in your daily shop matches one you have added to your wishlist, a ⭐ appears before the skin name. See [Gallery & wishlist](/features/gallery-wishlist) for how to manage your wishlist.

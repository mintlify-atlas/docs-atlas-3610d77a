The profile screen gives you a summary of your Valorant account at a glance — identity, progression, and currency balances all in one place.

## Account identity

At the top of the screen, VShop displays:

- **Avatar** — a circle showing the first two letters of your in-game name.
- **Display name** — your Riot `GameName`, fetched from the Riot name-service API at login.

## Account info

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Region | The Riot region your account is registered to (e.g. EU, NA, AP, KR). |

## Progression

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Level | Your current Valorant account level, sourced from the Account XP endpoint. |
| XP | Your current XP within the active level. |

## Currency balances

VShop shows all four in-game currency balances fetched from the Riot wallet endpoint:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Valorant Points (VP)" icon="coins">
    The premium currency used to purchase skins and bundles in the store.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Radianite Points (RAD)" icon="gem">
    Used to upgrade owned weapon skins to higher levels.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Kingdom Credits (KC)" icon="credit-card">
    Earned through gameplay; used to purchase items in the accessory shop.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Free Agents (FAG)" icon="user-group">
    Currency used for unlocking agents through the in-game agent recruitment system.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Balances are fetched when you log in and are not updated in real time. Pull down to refresh the app if you need up-to-date balances after making a purchase.
</Note>

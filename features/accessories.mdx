The accessory shop is a rotating selection of non-weapon cosmetics available for purchase with **Kingdom Credits (KC)**. It resets on its own independent timer, separate from the daily skin shop.

## Item types

The accessory shop can contain any combination of the following item types:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Gun buddies" icon="star">
    Small charms that attach to your weapon. Displayed with the buddy's icon image.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sprays" icon="spray-can">
    In-game spray tags. Displayed with the spray's full transparent icon.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Player cards" icon="id-card">
    Profile card artwork. Displayed using the card's large art image.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Player titles" icon="text">
    Text titles shown on your player card. No icon — only the title text is displayed.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Pricing

All accessory shop items are priced in **Kingdom Credits (KC)**. Kingdom Credits are earned by completing daily and weekly missions, and from the free track of the Battle Pass.

<Info>
  Kingdom Credits cannot be purchased directly. They are earned in-game through play.
</Info>

## Countdown timer

The timer in the top-right corner counts down to the next accessory shop reset. The remaining duration comes from the `AccessoryStoreRemainingDurationInSeconds` field in the Riot storefront response.

<Note>
  The accessory shop resets on its own schedule and is not tied to the daily skin shop reset.
</Note>

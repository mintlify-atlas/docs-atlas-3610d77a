---
title: "First-time setup"
description: "Complete the VShop onboarding wizard: choose your region and sign in with your Riot Games account."
---

When you open VShop for the first time, a three-screen onboarding wizard guides you through configuration. The app checks for a stored region on launch — if none is found, it routes you here automatically.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Welcome screen">
    The first screen displays the VShop app preview and a short introduction. No action is required on this screen.

    Tap **Next** to continue to region selection.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your region">
    Choose the region that matches your Riot Games account.

    <Note>
      Your region determines which store data VShop fetches. Selecting the wrong region will result in incorrect or missing shop items. Your selection is saved to local storage under the key `region`.
    </Note>

    The following regions are available:

    | Region | Code |
    |--------|------|
    | Europe | `EU` |
    | North America | `NA` |
    | Asia Pacific | `AP` |
    | Korea | `KR` |

    Select your region using the radio buttons, then tap **Next**.

    <Warning>
      The **Next** button remains disabled until you select a region. You cannot proceed to sign-in without making a selection.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in with Riot Games">
    The final screen loads the official Riot Games login page inside a WebView.

    Enter your Riot Games credentials and complete any two-factor verification if prompted. VShop does not handle or store your credentials — they are submitted directly to Riot's authentication servers.

    <Note>
      After a successful login, Riot redirects back to VShop with an access token. VShop extracts this token from the redirect URI to authenticate future requests. Your credentials never pass through VShop's code.
    </Note>

    Once authentication completes, the app navigates you to the main interface.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Returning users

On subsequent launches, VShop reads the `region` key from AsyncStorage. If a region is found, the app skips setup and routes directly to the re-authentication screen instead.

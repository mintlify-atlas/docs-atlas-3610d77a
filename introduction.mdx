# Welcome to VShop

VShop is a free, open-source Android app that lets Valorant players check their daily skin shop, Night Market, bundles, accessories, and profile — all from their phone. Your credentials never leave your device; VShop communicates directly with the official Riot Games servers.

## Privacy first

VShop runs entirely on your device. When you sign in, your credentials are sent **directly** to the official Riot Games authentication servers — VShop's own servers never see your password. Session cookies are stored locally using React Native AsyncStorage and cleared when you log out.
